Jobster is a full-stack recruitment platform designed to connect freelancers with companies through role-based dashboards and streamlined hiring workflows. Freelancers can create professional profiles, showcase their skills, and explore job opportunities, while companies can publish vacancies, search and filter candidates, and manage interested applicants. The project demonstrates practical experience in full-stack development, user authentication, profile management, search functionality, and responsive interface design.
